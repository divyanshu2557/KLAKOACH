Delivery & Heritage Transit

Shipping Policy

At Klakoach, we understand that our artisan treasures require the utmost care during transit. We have partnered with premium logistics concierges to ensure that your acquired masterpieces arrive safely and in pristine condition.

1. Domestic Dispatch

We take pride in offering complimentary premium delivery to all addresses within India.

  • Processing Time: Every artefact is carefully inspected and hand-packaged. Please allow 2-4 business days for processing before dispatch.
  • Transit Time: Standard domestic delivery typically takes 3-7 business days, depending on your proximity to metropolitan centers.

2. International Voyages

Our treasures adorn homes globally. We offer secure international shipping using trusted global couriers.

  • Transit Time: International transit generally takes 7-14 business days.
  • Duties & Taxes: Please note that international shipments may be subject to customs duties and local taxes. These levies are the responsibility of the recipient.

3. Packaging & Care

Each piece is encased in bespoke packaging designed to protect fragile materials such as ceramics, woodwork, and brass. We use environmentally conscious materials where possible without compromising safety.

4. Tracking Your Voyage

Once your order has begun its journey, you will receive an email containing tracking insights. Our concierge team constantly monitors all shipments to ensure timely arrivals.
